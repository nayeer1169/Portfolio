Machine Learning Engineer!


A machine learning engineer is a programmer who designs and builds software that can automate artificial intelligence and machine learning models.
They work at the intersection of data science, statistics, mathematics, and computer science The role of a machine learning engineer is new, and there is
limited information on the job titles and career paths. However, the job of a machine learning engineer is similar to that of a data scientist, as both
work with large volumes of data, have strong data management skills, and can perform complex modeling on setting in dynamic data sets.
